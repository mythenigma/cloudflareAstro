## FAQ: Architecture & Interior Design Sharing

### How many project photos can I share per gallery?

You can upload up to 25 images per gallery. For larger projects, create multiple galleries organized by phase, room, or area.

### Can I create before/after comparison galleries?

Yes! Upload before photos first, then after photos. The gallery will display them in order, creating a clear before/after narrative.

### How do I protect unreleased design work?

Set strict viewing limits (20-30 views), use short expiration dates (7-14 days), disable downloads, and only share links with authorized stakeholders.

### Can clients download project photos?

Yes, you can enable downloads in gallery settings. This is useful for client review and approval processes.

### How long should I keep project galleries active?

It depends on the purpose:
- Active projects: 30-60 days
- Completed projects: 180-365 days
- Portfolio galleries: 365 days or unlimited
- Competition submissions: As required
