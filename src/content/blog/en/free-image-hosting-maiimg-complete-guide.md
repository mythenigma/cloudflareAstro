# Free Image Hosting: Complete Guide to Maiimg Image Hosting Service

<div class="intro-panel">
  <p>Use Maiimg when you need a free host that also supports controlled sharing, not only raw storage.</p>
</div>

![Maiimg Home](/maiimg/maiimg-head.png)

## Core Capabilities

- Up to 25 images per upload.
- Up to 50MB per image.
- Share link and QR code in one flow.
- View limits and expiry controls.
- Open statistics and quick gallery removal.

## Setup Workflow

1. Upload images for one clear purpose.
2. Configure policy (view limit, expiry, optional duration).
3. Generate link and QR code.
4. Share by audience channel.
5. Review open statistics and adjust if needed.

## Recommended Settings by Use Case

| Use case | View limit | Expiry | Downloads |
|---|---|---|---|
| Client preview | Low-medium | 7-30 days | Optional |
| Campaign assets | Medium-high | 30-90 days | Usually on |
| Event photo delivery | High | 7-30 days | Optional |
| Internal draft review | Medium | 7-14 days | Optional |

## Practical Operating Rules

- One gallery, one objective.
- Do not mix client and partner audiences in one link.
- Set expiry before first distribution.
- Revoke old temporary links.

## Bottom Line

Maiimg works best as free hosting plus policy control: faster delivery, clearer boundaries, and measurable follow-up.
