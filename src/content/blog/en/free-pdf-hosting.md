## Security Features for PDF Hosting

MaiPDF offers several security features to protect your PDF files:

- **Access Control**: Set passwords, limit view counts, and set expiration dates
- **Download Protection**: Prevent users from downloading PDF files
- **Dynamic Watermarks**: Add watermarks to track viewer identity
- **Access Tracking**: See who opened your PDF files, when, and from where

## QR Code Generation

MaiPDF automatically generates QR codes for your PDF files, making it easy to share them on physical materials like flyers, business cards, or posters.
