# Free Secure PDF Sharing Tool: Complete Guide

<div class="intro-panel">
  <p>Free PDF sharing does not have to mean losing control. This guide explains how to share documents at zero cost while protecting access, preventing unauthorized downloads, and tracking who opened your files.</p>
</div>

## What “secure” means for PDF sharing

Secure sharing is about control. The best tools let you decide who can view, how long they can access, and whether they can download or print.

## Core features to look for

- **Share links and QR codes**: instant access without large attachments.
- **Access limits**: set view counts or expiration times.
- **Download controls**: allow or block downloading.
- **Watermarks**: show viewer identity to deter leaks.
- **Access tracking**: see when and where a PDF was opened.

## Step-by-step: share a PDF for free and safely

1. Upload the PDF to the sharing tool.
2. Set access rules (password, view limits, expiration).
3. Enable dynamic watermarks if the file is sensitive.
4. Generate a share link or QR code.
5. Test the link before sending it out.

## Best practices

- Share links instead of attachments to avoid version chaos.
- Use expiration dates for time-sensitive documents.
- Pair watermarking with download restrictions for confidential files.
- Always run a test open on mobile and desktop.

## Common use cases

- **Sales proposals**: prevent forwarding and track engagement.
- **Training materials**: limit access to employees only.
- **Portfolios and resumes**: present a clean, safe link.
- **Event brochures**: use QR codes on printed materials.
