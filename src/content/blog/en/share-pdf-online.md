# Share PDF Online: Easy Document Sharing

Online sharing avoids attachment limits and keeps document access easier to manage.

## Simple workflow

1. Upload PDF.
2. Generate share link.
3. Set policy (expiry/open limit/download).
4. Share by channel.

## Why online links help

- Better mobile reading
- Cleaner version control
- Faster revoke and replacement

## Final takeaway

For most workflows, online link sharing is the practical default over attachments.
