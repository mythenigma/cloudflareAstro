# Faster PDF Loading: Better Sharing Performance

Performance is a delivery feature. Faster loading increases completion and reduces drop-off.

## Practical performance checklist

- Keep PDF export optimized for web/mobile
- Test first-page load on 4G
- Use link previews with clear expectations
- Recheck performance after content replacement

## Where it matters most

- Sales and proposal workflows
- Event documents and QR access
- Mobile-first audiences

## Final takeaway

Faster loading improves business outcomes when paired with clear distribution and access policy.
