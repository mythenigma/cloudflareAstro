# Easy PDF Sharing: Upload Once, Share Anywhere

Sharing a PDF should be as easy as sending a text message. No file-size warnings, no bounced emails, no "which version did I send?" confusion. With a link-based approach, you upload once and share the same link everywhere.

![MaiPDF Landing Page](/maipdf2026/Maipdf_LANDING_PAGE.png)

![Upload Share Flow](/maipdf2026/flowchart/en-upload-share-flow.svg)

## Why Link-Based Sharing Beats Attachments

Traditional email attachments create problems that compound over time:

- **Size limits** — most email providers cap attachments at 10–25 MB. A single high-res brochure can exceed that.
- **Version drift** — once an attachment lands in someone's inbox, you lose all control. Update the source file and nobody gets the new version unless you resend.
- **No tracking** — you have no idea who opened the file, when, or how many times.

Link-based sharing solves all three. The recipient clicks a URL, the document loads instantly in any browser, and you retain full control on the backend.

## How It Works in Three Steps

**1. Upload your PDF**
Drag the file onto [MaiPDF](https://www.maipdf.com) or click the upload button. Files up to 100 MB are accepted with no account required for basic sharing.

**2. Grab the link (and optional QR code)**
Once the upload completes, you get a clean, professional link plus a scannable QR — perfect for print materials, presentations, or chat messages.

![Link and QR Code Result](/maipdf2026/Result_of_qr_link.png)

**3. Share it**
Paste the link into email, Slack, WhatsApp, WeChat, social media posts, or anywhere else that accepts text. Recipients view the document in the browser — no downloads needed.
