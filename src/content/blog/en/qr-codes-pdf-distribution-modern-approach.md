# QR Codes for PDF Distribution: Practical Approach

QR codes are the fastest offline-to-online bridge for PDF access.

## Deployment workflow

1. Upload PDF and generate link.
2. Generate QR code.
3. Set expiry/open-limit/download policy.
4. Test scan on iOS/Android.
5. Place QR in print and digital channels.

## Best scenarios

- Event guides and schedules
- Product manuals and cards
- Retail and venue documents
- Training handouts

## Final takeaway

QR distribution works best with clear lifecycle policy and real-device scan testing.
