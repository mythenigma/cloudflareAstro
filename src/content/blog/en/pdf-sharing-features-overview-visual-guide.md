![PDF Sharing Features Overview](/diagram/en/features-overview.svg)

## What you’re looking at

- **Online sharing controls**: view limits, verification, tracking
- **Offline option**: a self-contained package for no‑internet environments
- **Link management**: replace file, disable link, read notifications

## A simple way to choose

- Pick **online** when you need **real-time control** and **analytics**
- Pick **offline** when you need a **single file** that can be distributed without relying on a network
