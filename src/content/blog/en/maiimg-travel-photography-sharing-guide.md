## FAQ: Travel Photography Sharing

### How many travel photos can I share per gallery?

You can upload up to 25 images per gallery. For longer trips, create multiple galleries organized by destination, day, or theme.

### Can I share photos while still traveling?

Yes! Upload photos from your phone or laptop, generate a link, and share immediately. No need to wait until you return home.

### How do I protect my travel photos?

Set viewing limits, use expiration dates, disable downloads if needed, and only share links with trusted people. Delete galleries when no longer needed.

### Can I use QR codes in travel journals?

Absolutely! Generate a QR code for your gallery and print it in your travel journal. Readers can scan to view the full photo gallery.

### How long should I keep travel galleries active?

It depends on your purpose:
- Family updates: 30-60 days
- Travel blogs: 180-365 days
- Portfolios: 365 days or unlimited
- Personal memories: As long as needed
