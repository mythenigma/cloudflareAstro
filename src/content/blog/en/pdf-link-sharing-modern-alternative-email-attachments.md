## üìß The Death of Email Attachments

![MaiPDF Professional Interface](/2025MayMaiPDF/Home_Landing.png)

**Why email attachments are failing modern businesses**:

### Technical Limitations
- **Size restrictions**: Most email providers cap at 25MB
- **Security vulnerabilities**: Attachments can carry malware
- **Storage issues**: Clutters inboxes and servers
- **Mobile problems**: Large files don't work well on phones
- **Version control**: No way to update shared documents

### User Experience Problems
- **Download required**: Recipients must save files locally
- **Software dependency**: Requires PDF readers
- **Storage management**: Files accumulate on devices
- **Sharing difficulty**: Hard to forward or reference
- **Access control**: No way to revoke access

### Business Impact
- **Professional appearance**: Attachments look outdated
- **Security risks**: Sensitive documents in email systems
- **Compliance issues**: Difficult to track document access
- **Efficiency loss**: Multiple steps to share simple documents
- **Analytics gap**: No data on document engagement
