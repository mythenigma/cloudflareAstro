# PDF QR Code Generation with MaiPDF

<div class="intro-panel">
  <p>MaiPDF allows you to generate QR codes for your PDF files, making it easy to share them on physical materials like flyers, business cards, or posters.</p>
</div>

## QR Code Generation in Three Steps

1. **Upload PDF**: Upload your PDF file to MaiPDF
2. **Generate QR Code**: The QR code is automatically generated
3. **Share**: Share the QR code on physical materials
