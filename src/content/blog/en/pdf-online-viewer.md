# PDF Online Viewer: Fast, Cross-Device Reading

Online viewing improves recipient experience and keeps document control centralized.

## Why use online viewing

- No local download needed for reading
- Better mobile and browser compatibility
- Centralized policy controls (expiry, limits, restrictions)

## Operational checklist

- Test first-page speed on mobile
- Confirm rendering on major browsers
- Apply control policy before distribution

## Final takeaway

Online viewers are best when paired with policy defaults and link lifecycle management.
