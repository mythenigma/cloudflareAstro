# PDF Share Online: Simple and Controlled

Online sharing should be fast to send and easy to govern.

## Simple workflow

1. Upload PDF.
2. Generate link.
3. Apply policy (expiry/open limit/download).
4. Share to recipients.

## Final takeaway

Online sharing is strongest when link controls are set before distribution.
