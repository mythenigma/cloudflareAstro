# PDF Link Generator: Professional Sharing Workflow

<div class="intro-panel">
  <p>Upload a PDF, configure your sharing rules, get a link and a QR code. That's the whole flow. MaiPDF generates the link immediately — no account required for the reader, no software to install.</p>
</div>

## The generation workflow

1. **Upload** — drag your PDF onto the [create-link page](https://www.maipdf.com/pdf/maipdf2026.html). Supports large files.
2. **Configure** — set open limit, expiry, download/print permissions, email verification, watermark.
3. **Generate** — click **Create Secure Link**. You get a URL and a QR code.
4. **Share** — paste the link in email, chat, or display the QR at an event or in a presentation.
5. **Track** — check access records in [Control Center](https://www.maipdf.com/6/control-center.html) anytime.

![Link and QR code generated immediately after upload](/maipdf2026/Result_of_qr_link.png)

## What the reader gets

The reader clicks the link and sees the PDF in a browser viewer — no login, no app install, no download prompt (unless you allowed downloads). Works on desktop, tablet, and phone.

## Settings presets by use case

| Use case | Download | Expiry | Open limit | Extras |
|----------|---------|--------|------------|--------|
| **Sales proposal** | Off | 30 days | 15 | Watermark on |
| **Contract review** | Off | 14 days | 10 | Email verify |
| **Team policy update** | Optional | 60 days | 200 | Domain allowlist |
| **Event handout** | On | 7 days | 500 | QR for display |
| **Portfolio / CV** | On | 90 days | None | — |

![Set all your rules before generating the link](/maipdf2026/MaiPDF_settings_expiration_telegram.png)

## After you generate the link

You're not locked in. From Control Center you can:

- **Change settings** — update expiry, open limit, download rules at any time
- **Swap the PDF** — replace the file behind the same URL (link doesn't change, readers always see the latest version)
- **Revoke** — kill the link immediately if needed
- **Check records** — see every open with timestamp and (if email verification is on) the reader's address

## Why generate a link instead of attaching the file

- No 25 MB attachment size cap
- One URL always shows the current version
- Can be revoked or updated after sending
- Access records show you who actually read it
- Reader opens it in the browser — no download friction
