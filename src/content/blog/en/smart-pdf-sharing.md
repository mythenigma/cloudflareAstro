# Smart PDF Sharing: Upload, Control, Send in 3 Steps

<div class="intro-panel">
  <p>Smart sharing means you know what the reader can do with your document, you can update it after sending, and you'll know whether it was actually opened. Here's the full workflow.</p>
</div>

## Step 1: Upload (once per document)

Drag your PDF onto [maipdf.com](https://maipdf.com). The upload takes a few seconds. One PDF can power multiple links — a public link with download and a restricted link without, for example. You don't need to upload multiple copies.

## Step 2: Configure access settings

Before you create the link, decide what the reader can do:

**Must decide:**
- Download on or off?
- View limit (max opens) or unlimited?
- Expiry date or open-ended?

**Optional but often useful:**
- Email verification — requires inbox confirmation before PDF loads
- Dynamic watermark — reader's ID on every page they see
- Domain allowlist — only readers from specific company domains can open

![Settings panel — configure everything before generating the link](/maipdf2026/MaiPDF_settings_expiration_telegram.png)

## Settings templates by use case

| Use case | Download | Limit | Expiry | Extras |
|----------|---------|-------|--------|--------|
| Sales proposal | Off | 20 | 30 days | Watermark |
| Client contract | Off | 10 | 14 days | Email gate |
| Team internal update | Optional | 100 | 60 days | — |
| Public announcement | On | None | 90 days | — |
| Paid content / report | Off | Per-reader | 30 days | Email gate + watermark |

## Step 3: Share and track

Once you create the link, you get:
- **A URL** to paste in email, chat, or embed anywhere
- **A QR code** to display in presentations or print on materials

Share it across any channel. Readers open the PDF immediately in their browser — no install, no login.

![Share link and QR code — works everywhere](/maipdf2026/show_off/shareandsend.png)

After sharing, track from [Control Center](https://www.maipdf.com/6/control-center.html):
- **Who opened it** — timestamps, and verified email if gate was on
- **How many times** — total opens vs your limit
- **When to follow up** — "opened 3 hours ago" tells you when to call

## After you've shared: what you can still change

Unlike attachments, the link stays live and configurable. From Control Center:
- **Update the file** — swap the PDF without resending the link
- **Change settings** — extend expiry, adjust view limit, toggle download
- **Revoke** — kill access immediately when the window closes
