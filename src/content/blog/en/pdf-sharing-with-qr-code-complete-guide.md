## Why Use QR Codes for PDF Sharing?

QR codes offer several advantages for PDF sharing:

- ✅ **No typing required** - Just scan and view
- ✅ **Works on any device** - Native camera support
- ✅ **Offline distribution** - Print QR codes on materials
- ✅ **Trackable** - Monitor scans and access
- ✅ **Perfect for events** - Conferences, classrooms, trade shows

## How to Generate QR Codes for PDFs

### Step 1: Upload Your PDF

Visit [MaiPDF.com](https://maipdf.com) and upload your PDF file. No registration required.

### Step 2: Get Your QR Code

After uploading, MaiPDF automatically generates:
- A shareable link
- A QR code image

### Step 3: Share the QR Code

Download the QR code and use it on:
- Printed materials (flyers, posters, business cards)
- Digital displays (presentations, websites)
- Social media posts

## Benefits of QR Code PDF Sharing

- **Instant access** - Recipients can view PDFs immediately
- **Mobile-friendly** - Perfect for smartphone users
- **No downloads** - View PDFs directly in browser
- **Access control** - Set viewing limits and expiration dates
- **Tracking** - Monitor who accessed your PDFs
