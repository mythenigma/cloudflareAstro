# Dynamic Watermarks on PDF: Chinese Language Document Protection

When sharing PDFs in Chinese-speaking markets — mainland China, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Singapore — dynamic watermarks serve a critical role. They deter unauthorized redistribution in environments where document sharing is culturally fast-paced and often happens through mobile platforms like WeChat.

![Watermark Example](/maipdf2026/dynamic_water_mark_example.jpg)

## Why Watermarks Matter More in High-Sharing Cultures

In markets where group chats and instant forwarding are the norm, a document shared with one person can reach hundreds within hours. Dynamic watermarks create accountability:

- The viewer sees their own identity on every page
- This discourages casual forwarding (their name is on it)
- If the document does leak, it's traceable

## How Dynamic Watermarks Work

Unlike static watermarks (a fixed logo or text baked into the PDF), dynamic watermarks are generated at view time:

| Static Watermark | Dynamic Watermark |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Same text for all viewers | Unique per viewer |
| Can be removed by PDF editing | Generated by the viewer platform |
| No identity tracking | Displays viewer's email, IP, or custom ID |
| One-time application | Applied on every page render |

MaiPDF generates dynamic watermarks automatically when the setting is enabled.

## Setting Up Watermarks for Chinese PDFs

1. Upload your Chinese-language PDF to [maipdf.com](https://www.maipdf.com)
2. Enable "Dynamic Watermark" in settings
3. Choose the watermark content:
   - Viewer's email address
   - IP address
   - Custom text (supports Chinese characters)

![Settings](/maipdf2026/MaiPDF_settings_expiration_telegram.png)

The watermark appears on every page when the document is viewed — rendered at view time, not burned into the file.

## Chinese Character Support

MaiPDF's watermark engine fully supports:
- Simplified Chinese (简体中文)
- Traditional Chinese (繁體中文)
- Mixed Chinese-English text
- Custom messages like "内部文件 - 禁止转发" (Internal document - do not forward)

## Use Cases

**Business contracts** — share draft contracts with partners; each party sees their own identity watermarked on every page.

**Financial reports** — distribute quarterly results to investors; the watermark deters screenshots being shared on investment forums.

**Product catalogs** — share pricing with distributors; customized watermarks prevent competitors from obtaining your pricing through indirect channels.

**Educational materials** — distribute exam papers or proprietary course content with student ID watermarks.

## Combining Watermarks with Other Controls

For maximum protection in high-sharing environments:

| Control | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Watermark | Identity tracking + deterrence |
| View limit | Cap total access |
| Download off | No local copies |
| Expiration | Time-limited access |

## QR Code Sharing with Watermarks

A common pattern in Chinese markets: share a QR code (on WeChat, printed materials, or presentations). When scanned, the document opens with the viewer's information watermarked on every page.

![QR Sharing](/maipdf2026/flowchart/en-qr-sharing-flow.svg)
