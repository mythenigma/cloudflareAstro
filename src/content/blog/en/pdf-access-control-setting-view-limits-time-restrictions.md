## üî¢ Understanding View Limits

![Upload Interface with Access Controls](/2025MayMaiPDF/upload_in_cloudshare.png)

### Types of View Limits

**One-Time Access**:
- **Single view**: Document can only be opened once
- **Perfect for**: Sensitive information, one-time sharing
- **Use cases**: Confidential reports, personal documents
- **Security level**: Maximum protection

**Limited Views**:
- **Specific count**: Set exact number of views (e.g., 5, 10, 50)
- **Perfect for**: Controlled distribution, team sharing
- **Use cases**: Project documents, training materials
- **Security level**: High protection with flexibility

**Unlimited Views**:
- **No restrictions**: Document can be viewed indefinitely
- **Perfect for**: Public information, marketing materials
- **Use cases**: Product catalogs, public reports
- **Security level**: Basic protection

### Strategic View Limit Planning

**Assessing Your Needs**:
- **Document sensitivity**: How confidential is the content?
- **Audience size**: How many people need access?
- **Distribution method**: How will the document be shared?
- **Update frequency**: How often will content change?

**Best Practices**:
- **Start conservative**: Begin with lower limits, increase as needed
- **Monitor usage**: Track how many views are actually used
- **Regular reviews**: Adjust limits based on actual usage patterns
- **Document types**: Different limits for different content types
