# MaiImg Real Estate Image Sharing Guide

Real estate runs on images. Buyers decide which properties to visit based on photos long before they step inside. MaiImg helps agents share property visuals professionally, track buyer interest, and control distribution.

![MaiImg Platform](/maiimg/maiimg-head.png)

## The Real Estate Image Challenge

- **Large file sizes** — Email attachments bounce; MLS uploads compress quality.
- **No tracking** — Agent doesn't know which listings get the most photo views.
- **Uncontrolled sharing** — Competitor agents can use your photos without permission.
- **Outdated images** — Sold listing photos still circulate months later.
- **Mobile experience** — Buyers browse on phones; emailed images are hard to view.

## How MaiImg Solves Each Problem

### Professional Quality, No Compression
Upload original high-resolution photos to [MaiImg](https://www.maiimg.com). Viewers see the full-quality image in their browser — no compression, no downloads needed.

### Buyer Engagement Tracking
Every view is logged:
- How many people viewed each listing's photos
- When viewership peaks (after open houses? after new listing emails?)
- Which photos get the most attention

![Tracking](/maiimg/result_tracking.png)

### Controlled Distribution
- Set download to OFF if you don't want other agents using your photos
- Add expiration dates tied to listing contract periods
- Use view limits for exclusive preview sharing

### Always Current
Replace images without changing the link:
- Staged photos replace empty-room shots: same link, better images
- Price change banner added to photos: instant update
- Listing sold? Replace with "SOLD" overlay or disable the link entirely

### Mobile-First Viewing
Every MaiImg link renders beautifully on phones. Buyers scan a QR code on a yard sign and immediately see a gallery of photos — no app download, no pinch-to-zoom PDFs.

## Real Estate QR Code Workflows

### Yard Sign → Property Gallery
Print a QR code on your yard sign. Drive-by buyers scan with their phone and see all property photos instantly.

### Open House → Follow-Up
Display QR codes at the open house (on flyers and at key rooms). Interested buyers scan to save and review photos later. You track which visitors engaged most.

### Listing Flyer → Digital Gallery
Print listings with a QR code that opens the full photo gallery. More impactful than printing 4 small photos on a flyer.

### Email → Interactive Showcase
Instead of attaching 20 photos (and hitting email size limits), include a single MaiImg link. Recipients click once to browse all property images.

## Settings for Real Estate

**Active listing (public)** — Download on, high/unlimited views, expires at listing end date.

**Exclusive preview** — Download off, 50 views max, expires in 48 hours.

**Sold listing** — Download off, link disabled immediately.

**Agent portfolio** — Download on, unlimited views, no expiration.

![Settings](/maiimg/maiimg-setting.png)

## Measuring Listing Appeal

MaiImg analytics reveal which listings generate the most visual interest:

- **High view count** = strong buyer interest → prioritize follow-up
- **Low view count** = poor marketing reach → adjust distribution strategy
- **High views, no inquiries** = photos attract but property doesn't convert → review pricing or staging
- **View spike after open house** = event drove engagement → schedule more open houses

## Protecting Your Photography Investment

Professional real estate photography costs $200–$500+ per listing. Protect that investment:

- Disable downloads to prevent competitor agents from stealing your photos
- Set expiration to listing period — photos auto-disable when the contract ends
- Track views to ensure your photography investment is driving engagement
