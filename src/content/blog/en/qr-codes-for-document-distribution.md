# QR Codes for Document Distribution: From Link to Scan

<div class="intro-panel">
  <p>QR codes remove friction: no typing, no copying, just scan and open.</p>
</div>

![QR code distribution workflow](/diagram/en/qr-code-distribution-workflow.svg)

## Where QR distribution works best

- Posters, flyers, packaging, business cards
- Events and classrooms
- Customer onboarding docs and manuals

## Keep it reliable

- Use short-lived links for sensitive documents
- Test the scan flow on iOS + Android before printing
