# MaiImg Product Launch Image Hub

A product launch generates a flood of visual assets — product shots, lifestyle photos, packaging images, press kit visuals. Managing who gets which images, in what resolution, and tracking whether they actually use them is a logistics challenge. MaiImg turns chaos into a controlled hub.

![MaiImg Platform](/maiimg/maiimg-head.png)

## The Product Launch Image Problem

| Challenge | What Goes Wrong |
|-----------|----------------|
| Version confusion | Retailer uses old product shot from 3 months ago |
| Premature leaks | Press publishes images before the embargo date |
| No tracking | Marketing has no idea who downloaded the assets |
| Format chaos | Different teams need different resolutions and crops |
| Revocation impossible | Once sent via email, images can't be recalled |

## Setting Up Your Image Hub

### 1. Upload All Assets
Upload product images to [MaiImg](https://www.maiimg.com) — supports PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, and WebP, up to 50 MB each, 25 files per batch.

### 2. Create Distribution Channels
Generate separate links for each audience:

| Audience | Controls | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| Press / media | Expiration = embargo date, download ON | Need high-res files, but not before the launch |
| Retail partners | View limit per partner, download ON | Need product shots for listings |
| Social media team | No download, view only | Preview before final approval |
| Internal review | View limit, watermark ON | Prevent premature sharing |

### 3. Distribute Links
Send each audience their specific link:
- **Press**: include in press kit email
- **Retailers**: send via partner portal or direct email
- **Social team**: share in the team channel

### 4. Monitor Engagement

![Tracking Dashboard](/maiimg/result_tracking.png)

Track:
- Which press outlets accessed the images (and when — before or after embargo?)
- Which retailers downloaded product shots
- Whether the social team reviewed all approved visuals

## Pre-Launch Workflow

**Week -4**: Upload all product images with strict access controls (internal team only)

**Week -2**: Share with press under embargo (expiration set to launch day)

**Week -1**: Share with retail partners for listing preparation

**Launch day**: Remove restrictions, enable wider distribution

**Post-launch**: Review analytics, identify which images drove the most engagement

## Image Replacement Without Changing Links

Product images evolve during the launch process — colors get adjusted, backgrounds change, new angles are added. MaiImg lets you replace the image behind an existing link:

- The URL stays the same
- All existing recipients see the updated image
- No need to re-send links or update partner portals

Catch a color accuracy issue the day before launch? Replace the image — every shared link instantly shows the corrected version.

## Access Control for Competitive Protection

Product images are competitive intelligence. Competitors study your packaging, pricing displays, and product positioning through images. MaiImg's controls prevent unauthorized access:

- **View limits** ensure images can't be scraped en masse
- **Expiration** means leaked links become dead links
- **Watermarks** trace any unauthorized sharing back to the source
- **Download prevention** stops competitors from building a catalog of your visuals
