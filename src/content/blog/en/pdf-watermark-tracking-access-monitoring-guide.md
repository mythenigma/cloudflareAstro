## Why PDF Watermark and Access Tracking Matters

Sharing PDFs without tracking creates security and compliance issues:

**Problems Without Tracking:**
- Don't know who accessed documents
- Can't track viewing activity
- No accountability
- Compliance violations
- Security breaches undetected
- No audit trail

**Watermark and tracking solve these problems** by providing:

- ✅ Provide accountability

- ✅ Detect security issues
- ✅ Complete audit trail

## Understanding PDF Watermarks

### What Are Dynamic Watermarks?

**Dynamic watermarks** display unique information for each viewer:

**Watermark Information:**
- Viewer's email address
- IP address
- Access timestamp
- Custom text
- Unique identifier

**Benefits:**
- Track individual access
- Deter unauthorized sharing
- Provide accountability
- Support compliance

### Types of Watermarks

**Static Watermarks:**
- Same for all viewers
- Basic protection
- Limited tracking
- Simple implementation

**Dynamic Watermarks:**
- Unique per viewer
- Advanced tracking
- Better security
- Comprehensive protection
