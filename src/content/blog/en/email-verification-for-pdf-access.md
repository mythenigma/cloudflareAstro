# Email Verification for PDF Access (Quick Flow)

<div class="intro-panel">
  <p>Email verification is a lightweight way to reduce forwarded-link access without turning sharing into a complicated process.</p>
</div>

![Email verification access control workflow](/diagram/en/email-verification-access-control-workflow.svg)

## The flow (fast)

- Viewer enters an email
- Receives a one-time code
- Verifies to open the PDF

## Best for

- Proposals, quotes, and client deliverables
- HR and internal policy documents
